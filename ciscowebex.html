How to Make a Payment

  1. Log in to your CreditorWatch account.
  2. Navigate to Billing or Payments section.
  3. Select invoice or statement to pay.
  4. Choose payment method: Credit Card, Debit Card, or Bank Transfer.
  5. Enter payment details and confirm transaction.
  6. Receive payment confirmation by email.

Common Payment Issues

  • Payment not processing: Check card details or sufficient balance.
  • Duplicate charges: Contact support for immediate resolution.
  • No confirmation email: Check spam/junk folder or email address entered.
  • Cannot find invoice: Make sure you are in the correct account and billing period.

Payment FAQ

Can I pay invoices in installments?

Installment options depend on your agreement. Contact support for more info.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, AMEX, and bank transfer.

Where can I download past invoices?

Invoices are available in your account under Billing > Past Invoices.
